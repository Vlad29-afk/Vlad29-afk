About the Studio

Architecture that
listens before
it speaks.

Forma is a design-led architecture studio working at the intersection of material culture, landscape, and human experience. We believe that exceptional spaces emerge from careful listening — to clients, to context, to the land itself.

From private residences to civic institutions, each project is approached as a singular conversation between structure and site.
